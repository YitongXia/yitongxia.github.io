Current Position:

I am a PhD candidate at the Public Governance Institute and the Division of Geography and Tourism at KU Leuven, Belgium. I am currently engaging in an FWO-funded project "Mapping Polycentric Urban Development" where I mainly focus on developing scientific geocomputational tools to analyse and map urban polycentricity.

I also have a strong interest in programming and the development of open-source geographical software. I care about the open-source nature of software and data, and I am a strong advocate for open science. I am dedicated to exploring new possibilities within the fields of GIS and human geography through my programming skills.
